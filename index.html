Our Theme

STEM, ART AND INNOVATION

Our exhibition celebrates the convergence of Science, Technology, Engineering, Mathematics, Art, and Innovation. Students explore interdisciplinary connections that bridge analytical thinking with creative expression.

Through hands-on experiments, artistic creations, technological innovations, and collaborative projects, our students demonstrate how STEM and Art work together to solve real-world challenges and inspire meaningful change in our community.

This integrated approach fosters critical thinking, creativity, and innovation, preparing students to become tomorrow's leaders in an increasingly interconnected world.

Key Focus Areas:

Scientific Research Creative Arts Technology Innovation Engineering Design

School Citizenship Program

At The Indian School, our Citizenship Programme empowers students with essential life skills and values such as compassion, commitment, respect and resourcefulness. It goes beyond academics to nurture responsible and aware individuals.

Through Classroom Curriculum sessions, motivational workshops and activities like Nukkad Natak, Shramdaan crafts and service visits to hospitals and senior homes, students gain a deeper understanding of their rights and social responsibilities.
